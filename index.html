pay attention!!! reason behind the name "ATERVA"

"Aterva" is divided into 5 parts, each with its own meaning: Aeternus means "eternal," symbolizing everlasting friendship. Astra means "star," representing the idea of being useful to others. Bellva comes from a Greek word meaning "beautiful." It refers to the beautiful memories made during school at MAN IC Gowa that will always be remembered. Elpida is a Greek word meaning "hope." Vier is a German word meaning "four." It represents hope for the 4th generation of MAN IC Gowa, as they were the fourth batch of students.ATERVA is divided into three groups: Calvelius Soulfact Nchafelic
